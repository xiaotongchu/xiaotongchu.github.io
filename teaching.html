Teaching

2025

Practical Training Psychological Research — University of Amsterdam [Bachelor]

I delivered tutorial lectures and guided students through weekly writing assignments, with a focus on critical thinking, constructing convincing arguments, and developing scientific writing skills. Specifically, I guided students as they refined predetermined topics into clear research questions, conducted literature reviews, and formulated hypotheses. I also supervised their work on writing method sections, analyzing simulated datasets, reporting results, and critically reflecting on their findings.

Psychobiology Bachelor Project — University of Amsterdam [Bachelor]

I served as second examiner for Bachelor theses in Psychology. In this role, I was responsible for evaluating the quality of students’ theses and collaborating with primary examiners to discuss assessments and finalize grades.

2023

Persuasion in Consumer Communication for Sustainability — Wageningen University & Research [Bachelor]

I tutored this project-based course in which students collaborated with sustainability marketing practitioners from NGOs and government organizations. I guided students as they developed group reports and prepared presentations for case owners focused on improving marketing practices for sustainable living. I facilitated group discussions, provided structured feedback on their analyses and written work, and helped them refine and strengthen their persuasion strategies.

2022

Introduction to Strategic Communication — Wageningen University & Research [Bachelor]

I delivered tutorial lectures on strategic communication principles and theories, supervised group essay writing to guide students in analyzing communication phenomena, and facilitated discussions to ensure active student engagement and understanding. I managed course materials, assignments and assessments to enhance students' research and information skills.

Research Practice Seminar — University of Amsterdam [Pre-master]

I designed course content based on my own research, delivered tutorial lectures, and guided students through the full research cycle, from research design and operationalisation of concepts to data collection, analysis, and academic writing. I supervised students in conducting independent, academic‑level research projects and provided them with hands‑on preparation for advanced study in Communication Science.
